About Us

Company Overview

Equityhub is a dynamic private equity firm company based in Sandton and Durban, South Africa. We offer individuals, Families, groups of societies, churches and businesses worldwide opportunities to invest in diverse projects. Our platform provides a user-friendly experience , securing client funds and investing in carefully selected projects.
